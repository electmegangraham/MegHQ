V4 is the final redesign target for Megan Campaign HQ.

A virtual campaign office with Megan at the center.

- Megan is final authority
- Campaign Manager drives execution
- Departments operate like a cohesive campaign staff
- Megan's Desk is the primary executive interface
- Staff Mode exists underneath as the structured operational surface
- Doctrine / Holy Page governs message, direction, and standards
- Quality control is a formal system
- Brainstorm / Development / Simulation handle ambiguity and edge cases
- World Awareness monitors the outside environment
- Social Media is a full operating department
- Everything should be researched and optimized for time, money, people, and opportunity
