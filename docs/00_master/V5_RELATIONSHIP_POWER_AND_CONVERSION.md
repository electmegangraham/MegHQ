# MeganCampaignHQ V5 Relationship Power And Conversion

## Status
Locked implementation-grade specification.

## Core Truth
Relationships move campaigns.
Power must be mapped.
Contact must convert into action.

---

# 1. Purpose

This subsystem defines:
- relationship scoring
- influence mapping
- priority tiers
- contact conversion
- follow-up cadence
- outcome tracking

---

# 2. Relationship Objects

Every tracked relationship must include:
- person
- organization if relevant
- role
- relationship strength
- influence level
- access path
- last contact
- next step

---

# 3. Relationship Strength

Strength score:
- 0 = unknown
- 1 = weak
- 2 = light familiarity
- 3 = active connection
- 4 = strong connection
- 5 = trusted relationship

---

# 4. Influence Score

Influence score:
- 0 = none
- 1 = limited
- 2 = local
- 3 = meaningful
- 4 = major
- 5 = decisive

---

# 5. Priority Tier

Tier is based on:
- influence
- access
- strategic value
- timing relevance

Tiers:
- Tier 1
- Tier 2
- Tier 3
- watchlist

---

# 6. Access Path

Each relationship must define:
- direct
- warm intro
- indirect
- no path yet

No fake access assumptions.

---

# 7. Conversion Pipeline

Pipeline:

1. signal
2. person identified
3. relationship classified
4. outreach action created
5. contact occurs
6. result logged
7. follow-up scheduled
8. conversion outcome updated

---

# 8. Conversion Outcomes

Possible outcomes:
- no response
- informational contact
- soft support
- hard support
- volunteer
- donor
- validator
- connector
- event attendee
- follow-up needed

---

# 9. Follow-Up Cadence

Follow-up timing depends on:
- priority tier
- relationship strength
- recent activity
- active opportunity window

No relationship should go dark without reason.

---

# 10. Power Mapping

Power map must show:
- who matters
- why they matter
- who connects to whom
- where leverage exists
- where gaps exist

---

# 11. Desk vs Staff

Desk:
- high-value relationship opportunities
- major introductions
- strategic validator moves

Staff Mode:
- full relationship list
- follow-up queue
- status tracking
- outreach history

---

# 12. Explainability

Every priority relationship must answer:
- why this person matters
- what the access path is
- what action should happen next

---

# 13. Failure Modes

- important person untracked
- no follow-up after contact
- false relationship assumptions
- influence not recognized
- warm lead lost

Must be prevented.

---

# 14. Lock Statement

Relationship Power and Conversion turns network into movement.

Without it:
- influence is wasted
- follow-up collapses
- visibility does not convert
