# MeganCampaignHQ V5 World Awareness And Field

## Status
Locked V5 implementation-grade specification.

## Core Truth
The campaign exists in the real world.

This system ensures:
- external reality is tracked
- field activity is captured
- timing is understood
- opportunities and risks are not missed

---

# 1. Purpose

This subsystem exists to:
- monitor external signals
- capture field activity
- detect timing windows
- surface risks early
- surface opportunities early
- feed Campaign Manager with real-world context

---

# 2. Input Sources

- field interactions
- events
- community conversations
- media coverage
- opponent activity
- voter sentiment
- external news
- social signals
- stakeholder feedback

---

# 3. Field Capture

Field inputs must capture:
- who
- where
- what happened
- sentiment
- follow-up needed

No vague notes.

---

# 4. Signal Types

- risk
- opportunity
- sentiment shift
- issue emergence
- relationship change
- timing trigger

Each must be classified.

---

# 5. Routing Rules

Signals must be routed to:
- Campaign Manager (if executive relevant)
- appropriate department (if operational)
- storage (if low priority but useful)

Never left unclassified.

---

# 6. Timing Awareness

System must track:
- upcoming events
- deadlines
- media cycles
- voter attention windows

Timing affects priority.

---

# 7. Opportunity Detection

Opportunities include:
- event leverage
- media openings
- relationship introductions
- message alignment moments

Must be surfaced early.

---

# 8. Risk Detection

Risks include:
- negative sentiment
- opponent moves
- compliance exposure
- missed deadlines

Must be escalated when needed.

---

# 9. Field Feedback Loop

Field → system → action → follow-up → field

Must be continuous.

No dead ends.

---

# 10. Desk vs Staff

Desk:
- major risks
- major opportunities
- key timing moments

Staff Mode:
- raw field logs
- detailed signals
- tracking

---

# 11. Noise Control

Do not:
- surface every signal
- treat all input equally
- overwhelm decision layer

Manager filters.

---

# 12. Audit Rules

Track:
- source of signal
- classification
- routing decision
- action taken

---

# 13. Failure Modes

- missed opportunity
- ignored risk
- untracked field insight
- delayed response to signal

All must be minimized.

---

# 14. Lock Statement

World Awareness and Field connects the campaign to reality.

Without it:
- timing is missed
- risks grow unseen
- opportunities are lost

This system feeds the engine.
