# V4 Master Architecture

## Purpose
This document is the top-level architecture lock for Megan Campaign HQ V4.

V4 is not a dashboard, simple CRM, or task manager.
V4 is a virtual campaign office.

## Core truths
- Megan is the boss and final authority
- Campaign Manager drives day-to-day motion
- Departments act like skilled staff units
- Departments collaborate and reuse each other's outputs
- Megan's Desk is the primary executive experience
- Staff Mode exists as the structured operations surface
- Doctrine / Holy Page is the governing truth layer
- The system must be proactive, not shallow
- The system must optimize time, money, people, and strategic opportunity
