# V4 Design Principles

## Purpose
This file records the non-negotiable design principles of Megan Campaign HQ V4.

---

## Principles

### 1. Megan is the boss
The Campaign Manager drives, but Megan always has final authority.

### 2. Desk-first, not dashboard-first
The primary surface is Megan's Desk.

### 3. Conversation-first
Voice and typing are first-class ways to operate the system.

### 4. Hidden complexity
Operational dashboards exist, but remain secondary and hidden by default.

### 5. Manager-driven UI
The Campaign Manager is not just a brain. It is the primary interface conductor.

### 6. No weak output reaches Megan
Quality must be enforced systemically.

### 7. Departments must collaborate
Outputs from one department should become inputs for another where relevant.

### 8. AI + human hybrid by design
Seats and departments must support real humans, AI, or hybrid operation.

### 9. Research and optimization everywhere
Time, money, people, and attention should be used efficiently.

### 10. Nothing lives only in chat
If it affects behavior, workflow, UI, quality, decision-making, or collaboration, it belongs in the repo.
